# Junk Removal Businesses

Junk removal businesses are local hauling services that can start with simple equipment and grow through trust, realtor/referral relationships, professional presentation, search visibility, and monetizing removed items. In the Koerner Office corpus, the model is compelling because operators can charge for removal while also reselling, donating, or scrapping selected items.

## Patterns

- Referral routes matter: realtors, maids, handymen, property managers, and competitors with overflow.
- Professionalism increases pricing power: uniforms/branding, price sheets, photos, and customer conversation.
- Learn basic SEO, website, and ads before outsourcing.
- Equipment can scale gradually from pickup to trailer to dump truck.
- High-ticket cleanouts can be profitable but operationally intense.

## Revenue levers

- Removal fees.
- Resale of furniture or usable items.
- Scrap metal recovery.
- Donations and disposal optimization.
- Large hoarder/estate cleanouts.
