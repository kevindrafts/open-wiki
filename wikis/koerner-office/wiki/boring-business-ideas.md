# Boring Business Ideas

Boring business ideas are simple, often local or operationally unglamorous businesses with clear demand, low novelty risk, and room for better execution. Across Koerner Office episodes, the best examples usually win through location, speed, packaging, recurring revenue, or tedious logistics rather than invention.

## Examples in this corpus

- kegged iced/nitro coffee carts at events and high-traffic locations
- indoor golf simulator memberships
- rank-and-rent local lead generation
- corporate gifting logistics
- farmers-market packaged food products
- concrete coatings and other local home services

## Why they work

- customers already understand the purchase
- operations can be tested locally before scaling
- simple offers make sales easier
- tedious details become a moat
