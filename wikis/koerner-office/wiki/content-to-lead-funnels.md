# Content-to-Lead Funnels

Content-to-lead funnels convert short-form attention into owned contacts by pairing a retention-first post with a comment trigger, automated DM, landing page, and email/phone follow-up. The Koerner pattern is especially useful for creators and local-service operators because old posts can keep acting like many small evergreen lead magnets.

## Playbook

1. Publish a niche-consistent short-form post with a strong hook.
2. Use an early CTA tied to a trigger word.
3. Send a DM with a button/link to a lead magnet.
4. Capture email first; collect phone only with clear consent.
5. Deliver the resource with a concise email.
6. Add follow-up workflows or AI voice demos where appropriate.

## Lead magnets

- PDF business plans or checklists
- audits and calculators
- tiny apps built with AI/vibe coding
- niche-specific scripts/templates

## Risks

Automation needs varied language and platform-safe pacing; phone/SMS follow-up has regulatory constraints. Related: [[sales-and-distribution-playbooks]], [[white-label-vertical-saas]], [[home-service-ai-lead-capture]].
