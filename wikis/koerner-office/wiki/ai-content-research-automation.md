# AI Content Research Automation

AI content research automation uses tools such as n8n, RSS feeds, Google Sheets, Gmail, and AI summarization to monitor niche sources and surface publishable ideas without manual daily research. The goal is to support ethical inspiration, reaction, and curation—not to copy full work from other creators.

## Patterns

- Track a defined set of high-signal channels or sources.
- Write source metadata into a simple database before adding AI layers.
- Aggregate notifications so the automation is useful rather than noisy.
- Package repeatable workflows as templates or setup services.
- Use the system to feed a consistent niche publishing cadence.

## Example workflow

1. Get the YouTube channel RSS feed from page source.
2. Use n8n RSS Read on a schedule.
3. Map publish date, channel, and title into Google Sheets.
4. Send a batched Gmail digest.
5. Add more channels and later summarize or tag ideas.
