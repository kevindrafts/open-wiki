# Physical Gifting for Sales

Physical gifting for sales uses memorable offline objects to earn prospect attention when email, DMs, and calls are saturated. In the Koerner Office examples, cakes, personalized boxes, and other gifts work because they create surprise, social sharing, and a reason for a warmer follow-up.

## Playbook

1. Choose prospects where customer lifetime value supports a high-touch gift.
2. Personalize the object enough to feel intentional.
3. Track delivery and follow up quickly.
4. Measure meeting lift and close-rate lift versus normal outbound.
5. Keep improving the gift as novelty decays.

## Constraints

The channel has logistics, compliance, and saturation risk. Some companies restrict gifts; some physical ideas will become overused.
