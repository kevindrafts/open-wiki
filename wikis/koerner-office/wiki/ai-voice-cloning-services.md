# AI Voice Cloning Services

AI voice cloning services package synthetic voice generation into practical deliverables: multilingual voicemails, local-business phone messages, audiobook narration, YouTube dubbing, and recurring audio updates. The Koerner Office pattern is to sell a concrete communication asset, not the underlying AI tool.

## Pattern

A good voice-cloning service needs:

- clear consent from the voice owner
- a repeatable script library
- a niche with frequent customer communication
- language/translation review
- clean file delivery
- clear disclosure/compliance handling

This links to [[ai-enabled-service-businesses]], [[local-service-lead-generation]], and [[sales-and-distribution-playbooks]].

## Example: multilingual realtor voicemail packages

From `episodes/2026-06-09-the-laziest-ai-side-hustle-anyone-can-start-right-now.md`: clone a realtor's voice, generate short voicemail scripts, translate them into relevant local languages, and deliver organized audio files for listing alerts, showing confirmations, price drops, reminders, and missed-appointment follow-ups.

## Possible niches

- realtors and broker offices
- insurance agents
- car dealerships
- immigration attorneys
- medical offices
- authors/self-publishers
- YouTubers and faceless channel operators
- local businesses with phone systems or ads

## Risks

- voice consent and impersonation ethics
- telecom, robocall, voicemail, and marketing compliance
- translation accuracy
- commercial licensing for AI voice tools
- disclosure requirements
- brand/trust damage from uncanny audio
