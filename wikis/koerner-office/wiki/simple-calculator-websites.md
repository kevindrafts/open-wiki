# Simple Calculator Websites

Simple calculator websites are tiny tools that answer one searchable question with a number, estimate, quiz result, or generated output. In the Koerner Office corpus, they are a software-light way to turn search demand into ads, affiliate clicks, email subscribers, or leads for providers in a niche.

## Pattern

A useful calculator usually has:

- a query people already search for
- a small number of obvious inputs
- an immediately useful output
- explanation/tips that add trust and SEO surface area
- a next step: email unlock, vendor lead, affiliate link, consultation, or saved report

This is a specific subset of [[tiny-tools-and-micro-apps]] and can feed [[local-service-lead-generation]].

## Example niches

- AI token/API cost estimator
- side-hustle profit calculator
- pet or horse cremation cost calculator
- local-service pricing estimators
- product margin or resale-profit calculators

## Operating principles

1. Validate search demand before building.
2. Make the calculation transparent enough to trust.
3. Avoid blocking all value behind an email wall.
4. Use disclaimers for regulated or highly variable estimates.
5. Reuse the template only after one niche shows acquisition signal.
