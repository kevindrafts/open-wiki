# Indoor Golf Simulator Memberships

Indoor golf simulator memberships monetize expensive equipment that would otherwise sit idle by placing it in a small, self-serve local facility with recurring member access. The model depends on convenience, reliable access control, scheduling, and enough nearby golfers rather than broad retail foot traffic.

## Model

- lease a small space near neighborhoods
- install simulator hardware, cameras, and door access
- offer recurring membership tiers and punch cards
- keep the location largely self-serve
- use early members for feedback before heavy marketing

## Unit economics to test

Important variables include rent, leasehold improvements, simulator capex, insurance, utilities, scheduling/access software, cleaning, and the number of members needed for break-even.
