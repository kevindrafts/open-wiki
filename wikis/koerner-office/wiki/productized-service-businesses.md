# Productized Service Businesses

Productized service businesses package a repeatable service into a fixed, easy-to-buy offer, often with subscription pricing and minimal custom scoping. The Koerner Office examples show that productization can turn design, copywriting, video clipping, AI implementation, and development support into high-margin solo or small-team businesses.

## Characteristics

- clear buyer and narrow outcome
- public pricing or simple subscription terms
- limited meetings and custom proposals
- recurring or pause/resume billing
- repeatable delivery process

## AI opportunity

AI increases demand for humans who can finish, polish, connect, host, and ship what nontechnical users prototype. Services around AI-first design/development can sell the last-mile work that generic tools do not handle well.
