# Farmers Market Product Launches

Farmers market product launches use existing weekend foot traffic to validate packaged products, gather live feedback, create content, and generate cash before investing heavily in ecommerce or retail. The best examples combine a demonstrable product, sampling, clear packaging, and careful market selection.

## Launch steps

- pick a product that can be sampled or demonstrated
- package it simply and legally under local food rules
- test multiple markets and record sales by location/event type
- film reactions where appropriate
- use the best-performing pitch and content for online ads

## What to watch

City-run and event-attached markets may outperform low-traffic roadside markets. Licensing, food safety, and market rules differ by place.
