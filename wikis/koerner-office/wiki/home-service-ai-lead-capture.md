# Home Service AI Lead Capture

Home service AI lead capture sells faster response to local service businesses that miss calls, respond slowly, or fail to qualify leads after hours. The Koerner Office pattern is to pair small tools, AI voice agents, and simple local outreach with urgent service niches where speed matters.

## Pattern

A home-service lead-capture product usually includes:

- an instant quote or intake form
- an AI voice agent or answering flow
- lead qualification questions
- urgent-call forwarding
- call summaries sent to the owner
- clear ROI tracking by booked job

## Example: garage door repair tools

From `episodes/2026-06-04-the-simplest-ai-side-hustle-for-beginners.md`: build an instant quote generator and job profit tracker with Lovable, create an inbound AI voice agent in GoHighLevel, scrape garage-door companies from Google Maps, and test interest with cold SMS.

## Tool stack from the episode

- ChatGPT for Lovable prompts
- Lovable for quote/profit-tracker apps
- GoHighLevel AI Employee for voice agent
- Outscraper for Google Maps data
- Google Sheets for cleaning leads
- Straight Text for SMS outreach

## Risks

- cold SMS/TCPA compliance
- scraped phone-number quality
- AI voice-agent reliability and disclosure
- emergency-call handling
- demand validation vs. actual paid conversion
- speculative recurring revenue math
