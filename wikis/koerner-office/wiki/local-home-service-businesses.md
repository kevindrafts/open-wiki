# Local Home Service Businesses

Local home service businesses sell practical improvements or urgent services to nearby homeowners and commercial customers. Koerner Office examples emphasize simple offers, strong local demand, fast lead response, visual proof, reviews, referrals, and paid local ads.

## Pattern

A strong local home-service opportunity often has:

- high-ticket or urgent jobs
- local buyer intent
- visual proof or before/after content
- a repeatable crew workflow
- review/referral loops
- fast response as a competitive advantage

This connects to [[local-service-lead-generation]], [[sales-and-distribution-playbooks]], [[boring-business-ideas]], and [[ai-enabled-service-businesses]].

## Example: concrete floor coatings

From `episodes/2026-06-07-he-made-60k-his-first-month-with-this-boring-business.md`: a garage floor coating business can use process videos, Meta ads, Google reviews, fast lead response, in-person estimates, deposits, and a repeatable one-day crew workflow.

## Acquisition levers

- Meta/Facebook ads with process videos
- Google Business Profile and reviews
- referrals with a cash incentive
- personal/community network for early jobs
- quick follow-up to every lead

## Risks

- licensing, insurance, and worker classification
- safety and chemical handling
- rework from bad prep or weather
- seasonality
- ad-cost variation by market
- self-reported revenue claims needing verification
