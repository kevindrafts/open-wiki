# Local Service Lead Generation

Local service lead generation creates demand capture systems for trades and service businesses that monetize calls quickly. Koerner Office examples include AI quote/voice-agent workflows, rank-and-rent websites, direct-mail QR funnels, and content-driven lead magnets for home-service offers.

## Common channels

- Google SEO and rank-and-rent sites
- direct mail and QR landing pages
- AI voice agents and quote forms
- Instagram/comment lead magnets for local offers
- review/referral loops

## Operating principle

Local providers care less about marketing theory than answered calls, booked jobs, and predictable cost. Proof, tracking, and speed matter more than polished branding.
