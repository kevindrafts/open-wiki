# PR-Led AI Consulting

PR-led AI consulting uses a compelling AI adoption story to create trust and inbound demand for implementation, training, or productized AI services. The Koerner Office pattern is that the distribution story can be more important than the initial software: local news, press releases, public building, partnerships, and repeated posting make the offer legible.

## Pattern

A PR-led AI consulting business usually combines:

- a simple AI-enabled product or service
- a human-interest story angle
- local or industry media outreach
- social proof from partners/customers
- daily public posting
- follow-up offers for training or implementation

## Acquisition levers

- local TV news
- podcasts
- press releases
- LinkedIn/social posting
- direct outreach to reporters and partners
- public progress updates
- institution/partner announcements

## Risks

- PR outcomes are not guaranteed or repeatable
- partnership/revenue claims need verification
- public claims require careful wording
- publicity creates leads only if there is a clear offer behind it
