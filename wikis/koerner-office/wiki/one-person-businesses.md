# One-Person Businesses

One-person businesses use narrow offers, automation, productization, or asset ownership so a single operator can generate meaningful revenue without building a large team. In the Koerner Office corpus, the strongest solo models avoid custom complexity and keep fulfillment either recurring, self-serve, or tightly scoped.

## Patterns

- productized service subscriptions such as design
- ranked lead-gen assets rented to providers
- AI-enabled agencies fulfilled by tools and templates
- membership facilities with self-serve access
- digital products built from expertise

## Design principles

- reduce custom scoping
- sell recurring outcomes
- automate scheduling/access/admin
- use tools to track proof and reduce disputes
- prefer simple operations over maximum revenue extraction
