# Corporate Gifting Services

Corporate gifting services handle the sourcing, personalization, packing, and shipping of gifts for prospects, clients, and internal teams. The opportunity is strongest when companies value memorable relationship-building but do not want employees spending expensive sales or executive time on logistics.

## Service components

- gift strategy around budget and recipient details
- wholesale sourcing and vendor relationships
- personalization, branding, and packaging
- fulfillment, shipping, and deadline management
- minimum order sizing to protect margins

## Acquisition

Referrals, LinkedIn posts, and recipient/client social proof can outperform paid ads because the gift itself is visual and shareable.
