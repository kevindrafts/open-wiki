# Claude Skills Products

Claude skills products turn repeated AI workflows into portable Markdown instruction packs that can be sold as digital products, used in consulting, or reused inside internal operating systems. The durable opportunity is not generic prompting; it is packaging real subject-matter expertise so users can get a better result faster across Claude Code, Codex, Gemini, Grok, or similar tools.

## Pattern

- Start with a workflow the operator already performs repeatedly in an LLM.
- Convert it into a structured skill/instruction file with examples, constraints, and context.
- Sell the packaged skill stack directly or use it as the delivery engine for a consulting offer.
- Keep the asset portable so it can survive model or platform changes.

## Good niches

- SEO/content production
- LinkedIn or short-form content analysis
- industry-specific research and reporting
- creator workflows
- operational checklists for small teams
