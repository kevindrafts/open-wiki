# Rank-and-Rent Local Lead Generation

Rank-and-rent local lead generation builds city/service websites, ranks them in Google, routes calls through owned tracking numbers, and rents the resulting lead flow to local providers. The sales advantage is that the operator can prove value with real calls before asking for a monthly fee.

## Core model

1. Find a local niche with search demand and weak competition.
2. Build a fast, clear website around one service and city.
3. Rank it with content, backlinks, and basic local SEO.
4. Add call tracking and lead forms.
5. Send calls to a provider for free until value is proven.
6. Charge a flat monthly fee and expand into nearby cities.

## Pricing notes

Flat monthly pricing is less precise than pay-per-lead but avoids constant disputes over call quality and invoice swings. Operators may deliberately underprice accounts to preserve passivity and retention.
