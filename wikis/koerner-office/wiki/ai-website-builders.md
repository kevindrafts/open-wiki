# AI Website Builders

AI website builders turn natural-language prompts into usable websites, lead forms, chat-style intake, design variations, and section revisions. The opportunity for solo agencies is not merely that websites can be generated quickly; it is that niche knowledge, taste, testing, and business outcomes can be layered on top of fast fulfillment.

## Patterns

- Pick a vertical the operator understands so prompts and review are better.
- Ask the tool what prompt structure it expects before writing the build prompt.
- Include target audience, business goal, credibility proof, required pages, lead capture, and design feel.
- Stress-test forms, inbox routing, mobile behavior, and unusual customer inputs.
- Use speed as a competitive advantage, but still charge for outcome and judgment.

## Good fits

Local restaurants, clubs, service businesses, small retailers, creator side projects, and MVP local landing pages.

## Caveats

Review accessibility, SEO, privacy, forms, image rights, brand rights, integrations, security, and copy accuracy before shipping a client site.
