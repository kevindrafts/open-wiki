## Operational playbook

1. Build or package a simple AI-enabled tool/service.
2. Create a clear human-interest story angle.
3. Pitch local news with why viewers should care.
4. Turn early wins into follow-up PR.
5. Use partner quotes/logos only with permission.
6. Post daily about the journey, lessons, tools, wins, and failures.
7. Keep taking asymmetric outreach bets.
