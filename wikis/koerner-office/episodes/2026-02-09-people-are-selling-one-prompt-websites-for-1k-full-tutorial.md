## Operational playbook

1. Choose an industry you know and enjoy.
2. Ask the AI tool what prompt structure it expects.
3. Give specific business goals, target audience, credibility elements, functions, and design preferences.
4. Stress-test lead capture and inbox routing.
5. Regenerate weak sections and manually refine moments-between-the-moments details.
6. Price based on outcome/speed, not just hours spent.
