## Source summary

This edited training combines Koerner's short-form content playbook with a GoHighLevel implementation walkthrough. The reusable system is: publish retention-first content, prompt comments with a clear trigger word, DM a lead magnet, capture email/phone, then automate follow-up with email, SMS, voice AI, or app-based lead magnets.

## Business ideas

- Build comment-to-DM lead funnels for creators and local businesses.
- Sell setup/maintenance of Instagram automation and lead magnets to SMBs.
- Use AI-built PDFs or small apps as higher-value lead magnets.
- Build AI voice-agent demos after a lead requests a resource.

- creators with short-form audiences

- affiliate/internet marketers

## Acquisition channels

- retention-first short-form video
- early/clear CTA tied to a trigger word
- Instagram comments and DMs
- lead magnets distributed by email
- evergreen old posts acting as many small lead magnets

## Operational steps

1. Choose one content niche and stay consistent enough for platform learning.
2. Use a hook and delayed reveal/"hide the idea" structure to increase retention.
3. Add an early CTA: comment a trigger word to get the plan/audit/resource.
4. Build a workflow: Instagram comment trigger, varied automated replies, DM button, landing page, email capture, thank-you email.
5. Layer follow-up: additional emails, SMS with consent, or AI voice agent demos.
6. Reuse each workflow by copying it and changing the trigger word/resource.

## Tools / AI workflows

## Caveats and claims to verify

- Platform automation can trigger spam filters if replies are repetitive or overly aggressive.
- Phone/SMS follow-up needs explicit consent and regulatory care.
- Koerner warns faceless content is possible but real faces/stories may matter more as AI content proliferates.
