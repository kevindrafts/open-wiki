## Summary

This live demo validates an [[home-service-ai-lead-capture|AI voice-agent]] offer by scraping local service leads, sending ringless voicemail drops, fielding callbacks, and building a GoHighLevel voice agent for a tree-trimming business. The main lesson is that fulfillment is becoming easy; the bottleneck is choosing a painful local use case, getting business owners to respond, and handling follow-up professionally.

## Concrete business ideas

- **AI voice-agent setup:** build answering/qualification agents for home services that miss calls after hours or while in the field.
- **Ringless voicemail prospecting:** test demand by dropping a short voicemail to hundreds of local businesses.
- **Lead scraping + validation:** use local category searches, Google Trends/keyword research, Outscraper-style lists, and phone validation.
- **Multi-touch outbound:** combine voice note, email, and text rather than relying on one channel.

## Customer segments

Tree services, HVAC contractors, gutter cleaners, pool services, contractors, veterinarians, and other field-service operators with urgent inbound calls.

## Acquisition channels mentioned or implied

Google Maps/Outscraper lead lists, Google Trends, keyword tools, ringless voicemail, Google Voice, texts, calls, email follow-up, and live proof demos.

## Operational playbook

1. Pick a home-service category where missed calls cost real jobs.
2. Build or buy a clean lead list with phone numbers.
3. Validate number type and upload a small compliant test batch.
4. Record a short voicemail focused on one pain point.
5. Prepare callback handling before launching.
6. Build the agent with a website crawl/knowledge base, required intake fields, notifications, and a test call.

## Caveats / claims to verify

Ringless voicemail, robocalls, texting, and scraped outreach can trigger TCPA, state-law, platform, and reputation issues. Operators should verify compliance and consent requirements before copying the tactic.
