## Summary

This tutorial shows how to build [[ai-content-research-automation]] with n8n, YouTube RSS feeds, Google Sheets, and Gmail so content creators can wake up to new niche-specific video ideas. Koerner frames the business opportunity as repurposing or reacting to content in a chosen niche, then monetizing the audience rather than manually hunting for inspiration every day.

## Concrete business ideas

- **Content inspiration automation:** monitor top YouTube channels and collect new videos into a Google Sheet.
- **Creator research service:** sell setup and maintenance of RSS/Sheets/email monitoring workflows to niche creators.
- **n8n template product:** package reusable automations for creators to buy and adapt.
- **Niche content business:** pick a subject, post consistently, build an audience, then monetize.

## Customer segments

Creators, newsletter operators, niche publishers, local-events accounts, parent-hack pages, motorcycle or power-washing channels, and content agencies.

## Acquisition channels mentioned or implied

Posting consistently in a niche, monitoring competitor channels, using RSS feeds, Google Sheets, Gmail alerts, prebuilt n8n templates, and selling workflow templates.

## Operational playbook

1. Pick the niche and list the channels to monitor.
2. Create a Google Cloud project and connect Google/YouTube/Sheets credentials.
3. Use a channel RSS feed as the trigger/source.
4. Write publish date, channel name, and video title into a sheet.
5. Add Gmail notifications, but aggregate items to avoid one email per video.
6. Expand to more channels and package the workflow once it is reliable.
