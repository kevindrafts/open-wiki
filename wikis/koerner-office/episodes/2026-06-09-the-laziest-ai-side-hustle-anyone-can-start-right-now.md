## Summary

This episode presents an [[ai-voice-cloning-services]] business: clone a service professional's voice, generate multilingual voicemail/audio messages, and sell them as one-time packages or recurring updates. The main example is realtors, but the same pattern can extend to insurance, legal, medical offices, YouTubers, audiobook authors, and local businesses.

## Concrete business ideas

- Multilingual realtor voicemail packages
- Recurring voice/audio support for listing alerts, price drops, reminders, and follow-ups
- Audiobook narration in an author's own cloned voice
- YouTube dubbing/localization services
- Local business voiceover agency for phone systems, ads, training, and announcements

## Customer segments

- Realtors and broker offices
- Insurance agents
- car dealerships
- immigration attorneys and other lawyers
- medical offices serving multilingual patients
- authors/self-publishers
- YouTubers and faceless channel operators
- local businesses needing phone/IVR/hold-message audio

## Pricing / revenue clues

- Realtor voicemail service: $200-$500/month suggested
- Example math: 5 realtors at $300/month = $1,500/month
- Setup fees: $500-$1,000 per client
- Local business audio projects: $200-$500/project
- Audiobook narration: $500 to a few thousand dollars, depending on scope
- Claimed production costs are low, but provider pricing/licensing should be verified

## Acquisition channels

- personal network of realtors/service professionals
- lunch demos with agents
- proactive voice-clone samples for prospects, with consent/compliance caution
- broker-office visits and short AI workshops
- one-page flyers with QR codes
- YouTube creator emails from channel pages
- freelancers/platforms for building targeted creator lead lists

## Operational playbook

1. Pick a niche with repeated voice/audio needs.
2. Get clear consent and a short voice sample.
3. Use an LLM to generate common scripts.
4. Translate scripts into target languages.
5. Generate audio with a voice-cloning/TTS tool.
6. Organize files by client, language, and message type.
7. Deliver a clean client folder.
8. Upsell recurring new-message generation or broader AI consulting.

## AI/tool workflow

- Fish Audio or similar voice-cloning/TTS tool
- Claude/ChatGPT/Gemini/Grok for scripts and translations
- Google Drive or similar delivery folder
- Optional CRM for recurring client requests

## Caveats / claims to verify

- Voice cloning requires consent and careful ethical handling.
- Cold calls, voicemails, and automated audio may trigger telecom/marketing compliance rules.
- Translations need accuracy and cultural review.
- Commercial licensing terms for voice tools should be checked.
- Disclosure requirements may apply for AI-generated voice.
